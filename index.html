The online kindergarten

Blossom is an online kindergarten where you can teach your kids with fun just like in any other kindergarten, but at the comfort and safety of your home.

Hey Blossom, How can I start teaching my kids here ?

Step 1:

Look for the yellow button that says "Start Teaching". You will see the button on the top of the page and at the bottom.

Step 2:

The yellow button will open a payment form which will ask you to make a payment. Once you pay you will get the full access of the teaching materials.

Step 3:

Now that you have all the things ready, it's time to have fun teaching your kids and spend some quality time with them.

Note : Hey! Mom and Dad, please don't get overexcited with this teaching thing, ok.
